💼 Service-Based Companies (TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant)

  • Focus Areas: Arrays, Strings, Basic Math (GCD, Primes), Searching/Sorting.
  • Recruitment: Bulk exams like TCS NQT or Infosys InfyTQ. Focus on time limits and speed.
  • Strategy: Master standard templates. Service companies test basic logic, syntax correctness, and edge cases. Solve 40-50 Easy & Medium problems.

🚀 Product-Based Companies (Zoho, Flipkart, Swiggy, Paytm, Zomato)

  • Focus Areas: Recursion, Backtracking (Zoho's favorite), Trees, Dynamic Programming, Graphs.
  • Recruitment: Zoho Round 2 (interactive console coding), Flipkart Machine Coding, Swiggy/Zomato technical interviews.
  • Strategy: Optimization is key (space/time complexity matters). Product-based companies verify code design, modularity, and clean coding. Solve 100+ Medium & Hard problems.
